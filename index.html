How We Pick What Makes the List

01

Bipartisan Coverage

The incident must have been covered by major outlets on both the left (CNN, MSNBC, NYT) and the right (Fox News, WSJ, Daily Wire). If only one side cared, it doesn't make the cut.

02

Mass Media Attention

It had to dominate at least one full news cycle. We're talking front-page, breaking-news, your-uncle-texted-you-about-it level coverage.

03

Sourced & Cited

Every entry links to video evidence, official documents, or primary reporting. No hearsay, no "some people say." Receipts or it didn't happen.
